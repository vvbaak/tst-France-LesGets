🚵 Thursday

25 June
Easy Riding – Les Gets Bike Park
  • Warm-up day – get familiar with the trails
  • Start with green & blue runs
  • Explore the Les Gets Bike Park flow trails
  • Lunch break at a mountain restaurant
  • Afternoon: progress to some red trails
  • Wind down and check bikes after riding
💡 Take it easy on day 1 – get your legs warmed up and learn the terrain.

🏔️ Friday

26 June
Portes du Soleil Bike Trip
  • Big day – the iconic Portes du Soleil MTB circuit
  • Cross-border ride between France & Switzerland
  • Take the lifts and ride the connecting trails
  • Pack extra water, snacks, and a rain jacket
  • Lunch at a mountain hut along the route
  • Return to Les Gets via the trail network
💡 Start early! The full Portes du Soleil loop is a long day. Bring a fully charged phone and trail map.

🤘 Saturday

27 June
Bike Park Riding – Send It!
  • Full day at the Les Gets / Morzine bike parks
  • Hit the jump lines and technical trails
  • Session your favourite runs from earlier in the week
  • Try some of the harder red/black trails
  • Afternoon: last laps before the lifts close
  • Evening: celebrate with dinner out 🍻
💡 Last full day of riding – make it count but stay safe. Check your brakes and tyre pressure!

🏠 Sunday

28 June
Departure Day – Les Gets → Netherlands
  • Pack up and clean the accommodation
  • Load bikes and luggage
  • Drive back to the Netherlands
  • Relive the memories on the drive home 🎵
💡 Check under beds and in bathrooms before you leave – things always get left behind!
